6th of Uktar

Okay so. The plan might have had a few flaws. Nothing major, but we did end up in a running[1] battle on the streets of Targos. Frankly, the “militia captain” (only thing this guy is captain of is being a jerk!!!!) was really to blame for this but it’s lucky that Cam was around to make peace[2] or things might have gone badly……… for him! Anyway Cam and Ace questioned the guy and got some info about some “blue eyed fuck” whose name I forget - because honestly I was furious at the captain- but which one of the others can add, who works for Torga.[3] 

I am completely fine with this whole “doing violence to terrible people” situation.[4] Doing really well. I know you probably don’t care about this as my employer or whatever, but I just thought it was important to get that on the record.

7th of Uktar

We decided to travel to Bremen as part of our general do-goodery tour, since it was close by and Targos maybe wasn’t the friendliest place to sleep at the moment. We met a naturalist by the name of Tully who, in a roundabout way, engaged us to investigate the lake monster (that I think is one of those water guys, from, you know, THE PAST - what’s it doing here, now? So crazy). We have observed due caution in assessing the situation, talking with sailors, obtaining swimming bladders etc, intend to go out on the lake and see what’s what in the morning. Current leading theory is that it’s some sort of evil third presence doing some mischief and possibly controlling the monster??? We’ll see! Either way, dealing with terrible monsters and investigating mysterious evil is definitely the stuff of heroes so mission accomplished there I guess! Or, it will be tomorrow!
